NAME

tally.control - keep track of newgroup creation and deletions.

SYNOPSIS

tally.control

DECSRIPTION

tally.control is normally invoked by scanlogs(8). It reads its standard input, which should be the newgroup.log and rmgroup.log log files. It updates the cumulative list of newsgroup creations and deletions, control.log.
