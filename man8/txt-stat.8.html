NAME

txt-stat - display the status of TXT

SYNOPSIS

txt-stat [--heap] [-h]

DESCRIPTION

txt-stat is used to display various information about the status of Intel(R) TXT. It will display the TXT configuration registers status and TBOOT log by default.

OPTIONS

--heap
Print out the BiosData structure from the TXT heap.
-h, --help
Print out this help message.

EXAMPLES

txt-stat --heap
2011-12-31 tboot
