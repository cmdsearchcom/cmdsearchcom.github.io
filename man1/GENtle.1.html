NAME

GENtle - program to plan genetic cloning experiments

SYNOPSIS

gentle

DESCRIPTION

GENtle integrates a series of programs and databases to plan the integration of foreign DNA into another larger DNA that by some means can replicate itself or is by some other means replicated - a process commonly referred to as cloning.

OPTIONS

None.
