NAME

gpsd_nmea.sh - switches your gpsd to NMEA mode

SYNOPSIS

gpsdrive [options]
 
 
This is needed for some Gps-Receivers with SIF Chipüset. If you don't switch them into NMEA mode the position will lag about 5-10 seconds behind the reality.
