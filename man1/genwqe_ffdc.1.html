NAME
genwqe_ffdc - IBM Hardware Accelerator Tool.

SYNOPSIS
genwqe_ffdc [-h] [-v,--verbose]

DESCRIPTION
Utility to do first failure data capture (FFDC).

[-C, --card <cardno>] [-Q, --dump-queues] Dump DDCB queue registers of all funcs [-V, --version] [-v, --verbose]
