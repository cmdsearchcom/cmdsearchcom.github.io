[-hi authhandle hierarchy (l, e, o, p)(default platform)] [-pwda authorization password (default empty)] [-pol policy file (default empty policy)] [-halg [sha1, sha256]
(default null)]

01 continue 20 command decrypt 40 response encrypt

SEE ALSO

should give you access to the complete manual.
