The Dhammapada is an anthology of 423 verses from the Kuddaka Nikaya part of the Theravada Pali Canon. It contains Buddhist ethical and spiritual precepts. One of the translaâ
tions is by John Richards; the other is an older translation by F. Max Muller.

displayâdhammapada and dhamma both print to the standard output.

âr Use the Richards translation (default).

With no arguments a random verse is printed.
