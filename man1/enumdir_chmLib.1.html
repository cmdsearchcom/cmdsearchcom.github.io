NAME
enumdir_chmLib - Lists the contents of chm file(s).

SYNOPSIS
enumdir_chmLib [FILE]

DESCRIPTION
enumdir_chmLib lists the contents of the specified chm file(s) to stdout.

USAGE
enumdir_chmLib <chmfile1> <chmfile2>...

OPTIONS
enumdir_chmLib has no options.
