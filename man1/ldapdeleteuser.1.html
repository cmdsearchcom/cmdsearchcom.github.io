NAME
ldapdeleteuser - deletes a POSIX user account from LDAP.

SYNOPSIS
ldapdeleteuser <username|uid>

OPTIONS
<username | uid>
The name or uid of the user to delete.

SEE ALSO
ldapdeletegroup(1), ldapdeletemachine(1), ldapscripts(5).
