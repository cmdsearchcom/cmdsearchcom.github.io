NAME
vlc-fbx - invoke vlc with correct options for the freebox

SYNOPSIS
vlc-fbx URL-to-play

DESCRIPTION
This manual page documents briefly the vlc-fbx command.

vlc-fbx is a wrapper around vlc so that video is played by the FreeBox

SEE ALSO
fbx-playlist(1).
