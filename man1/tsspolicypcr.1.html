NAME
tsspolicypcr - manual page for tsspolicypcr v713

DESCRIPTION
policypcr

Runs TPM2_PolicyPCR

-ha handle

-halg [sha1, sha256] (default sha256)

-bm pcr mask in hex

SEE ALSO
The full documentation for tsspolicypcr is maintained as a Texinfo manual. If the info and tsspolicypcr programs are properly installed at your site, the command

info tsspolicypcr

should give you access to the complete manual.
