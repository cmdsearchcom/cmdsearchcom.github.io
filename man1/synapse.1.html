NAME

synapse - Utility to launch everything on your computer.

SYNOPSIS

synapse [OPTIONS]
 

DESCRIPTION

Synapse is an utility to launch everything you have on your computer. It uses zeitgeist to search into the user's activity.
 

OPTIONS

-s, --startup=
Startup mode (hide the UI until activated).
