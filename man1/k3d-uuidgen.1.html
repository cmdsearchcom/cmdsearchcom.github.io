NAME
k3d-uuidgen - generates a unique identifier.

SYNOPSIS
k3d-uuidgen

DESCRIPTION
Generates a unique identifier for use by a new K-3D plugin. You can get similar results with other uuid tools, k3d-uuidgen is provided as a convenience.
