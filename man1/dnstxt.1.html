NAME

dnstxt - prints the TXT record of fqdn
 

SYNOPSIS

dnstxt fqdn
 

DESCRIPTION

dnstxt prints the TXT record of fqdn on a single line. If there is no TXT record, dnstxt prints a blank line.
 
Normally dnstxt exits 0. If dnstxt encounters a temporary problem that prevents it from determining the TXT record, it prints an error message and exits 111.
