NAME

wvPDF - convert msword documents to PDF

SYNOPSIS

wvPDF in_word_doc out_pdf_doc
 

DESCRIPTION

wvPDF converts word documents into Adobe's PDF format.

REQUIREMENTS

wvPS distill
