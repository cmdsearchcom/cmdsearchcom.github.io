NAME
idfcyl - generates an outline for a cylindrical component.

DESCRIPTION
idfcyl This program generates an outline for a cylindrical component. The cylinder may be horizontal or vertical. A horizontal cylinder may have wires at one or both ends. A verâ
tical cylinder may have at most one wire which may be placed on the left or right side. idfcyl is part of the KiCad suite.

SYNOPSIS
