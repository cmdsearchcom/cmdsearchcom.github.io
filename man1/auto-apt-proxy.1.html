NAME
auto-apt-proxy - autodetect common APT proxy setups

DESCRIPTION
auto-apt-proxy installs itself as a APT proxy autodetector, and detects common setups by checking localhost, your gateway and other "interesting" machines on your network for
well-known APT proxies such as apt-cacher-ng and others.
