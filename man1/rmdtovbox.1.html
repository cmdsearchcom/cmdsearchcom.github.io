NAME
rmdtovbox - isdn voice box (converter backend)

DESCRIPTION
This is the real converter, the backend. Donât call it directly; call it by one of its other names: autovbox, vboxtoau or vboxmode.

autovbox(1), vboxtoau(1), vboxmode(1), vbox(5)
