NAME

JargonInformatique - Front End for the french dictionary Jargon Informatique

SYNOPSIS

JargonInformatique

DESCRIPTION

Jargoninformatique is a french dictionary which describes essential words about computering (more than 10 000 words!). Its user-friendly interface lets you easily find the words you want.
