NAME
POFileEquiv - glossary generator

SYNOPSIS
POFileEquiv [OPTION] [...] [FILE] [...]

INTRODUCTION
POFileEquiv tries to generate a glossary based on a large enough sample of translations

COMMAND LINE OPTIONS
--help or -h
show usage instructions

--max or -m
maximum number of translations to consider

--occurences or -o
minimum number of occurences of a translation

--ratio or -r
minimum ratio to best match to include a word

MORE INFORMATION
