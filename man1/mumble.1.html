MUMBLE(1) General Commands Manual MUMBLE(1)

NAME

mumble - a low-latency, high quality voice chat program for gaming.

SYNOPSIS

mumble

DESCRIPTION

Mumble is a low-latency, high quality voice chat utility for gaming that runs under X11.

SEE ALSO

mumble-overlay(1), murmurd(1).
