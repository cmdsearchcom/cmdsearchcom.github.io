GBONDS(1) General Commands Manual GBONDS(1)

NAME
gbonds - U.S. savings bond inventory program for GNOME

SYNOPSIS
gbonds [options] files...

DESCRIPTION
GBonds is a savings bond inventory program for the GNOME desktop environment. It allows you to track the current redemption value and performance of your U.S. Savings Bonds and
keep a valuable record of the bonds you own. GBonds is similar in functionality to Savings Bond Wizard from the U.S. Department of the Treasury.

OPTIONS
-h, --help
Show summary of options.

-v, --version
Show the version of gbonds.

SEE ALSO
The GBonds manual, accessible from the Help menu within the application.
