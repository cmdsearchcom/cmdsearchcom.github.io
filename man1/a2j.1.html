NAME
a2j - Wrapper script to simulate a2jmidid’s non-DBUS behaviour though a2jmidid actually being in DBUS mode

SYNOPSIS
a2j [OPTIONS]

OPTIONS
-e Also export MIDI hardware ports, not only software.
