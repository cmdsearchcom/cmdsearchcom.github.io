NAME
focuswriter - fullscreen word processor

SYNOPSIS
focuswriter [FILENAME...]

DESCRIPTION
FocusWriter is a simple, distraction-free word processor. It utilizes a hide-away interface that you access by moving your mouse to the edges of the screen, allowing the program
to have a familiar look and feel to it while still getting out of the way so that you can immerse yourself in your work.
