NAME

mew-pinentry - simple pinentry for gpg-agent

SYNOPSIS

mew-pinentry

DESCRIPTION

The mew-pinentry utility is a simple pinentry for gpg-agent. It is a front end to ask a passphrase to a user.
You may configure "~/.gnupg/gpg-agent.conf" to tell "gpg-agent" the path.
