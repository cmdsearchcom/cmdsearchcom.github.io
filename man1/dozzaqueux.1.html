NAME
dozzaqueux - chemical simulator for aqueous mixtures

SYNOPSIS
dozzaqueux

DESCRIPTION
dozzaqueux is a program most useful to simulate aqueous solutions.

choose reactants to put in the beaker, those to put in the burette, dozzaqueux plots the concentrations vs. the mixed volume, and many more.
