NAME
dumpcpandist - show some details for a distribution from the CPAN

SYNOPSIS
dumpcpandist $filename [use_tar => ...]

Where $filename is a tarball or zipfile from the CPAN. The arguments are the same as CPAN::ParseDistribution’s "new()" method.

SEE ALSO
CPAN::ParseDistribution
