NAME

gisomount — GUI to handle the mounting of ISO images as virtual CDs

SYNOPSIS

gisomount

gisomount

gisomount loads a graphical interface for easily mounting iso images as virtual devices and can perform many other useful functions, such as md5sum, burning and browsing. Due to the nature of mount and the fact that pmount doesn't yet allow mounting of loopback devices, this program must be run as root.

SEE ALSO

mount(1).

AVAILABILITY

gisomount is available in source form from https://launchpad.net/products/gisomount (link to URL https://launchpad.net/products/gisomount) .
