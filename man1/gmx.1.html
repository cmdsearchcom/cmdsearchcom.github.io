NAME
gmx - molecular dynamics simulation suite

SYNOPSIS
gmx [-[no]h] [-[no]quiet] [-[no]version] [-[no]copyright] [-nice <int>]
[-[no]backup]

DESCRIPTION
GROMACS is a full-featured suite of programs to perform molecular dynamics simulations, i.e., to simulate the behavior of systems with hundreds to millions of particles using
Newtonian equations of motion. It is primarily used for research on proteins, lipids, and polymers, but can be applied to a wide variety of chemical and biological research
questions.

OPTIONS
Other options:

-[no]h (no)
Print help and quit

-[no]quiet (no)
Do not print common startup info or quotes

-[no]version (no)
Print extended version information and quit

-[no]copyright (yes)
Print copyright information on startup

-nice <int> (19)
Set the nicelevel (default depends on command)

-[no]backup (yes)
Write backups if output files exist
