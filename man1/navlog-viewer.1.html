NAME
navlog-viewer - GUI viewer of robot navigation logs

SYNOPSIS
navlog-viewer

DESCRIPTION
A GUI application for playing back the logs generated by programs such as GridmapNavSimul. They are useful to debug the internal workings of robot navigation algorithms, their
internal decisions, etc.
