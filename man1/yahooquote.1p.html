NAME
yahooquote - Get stock quotes from Yahoo!

SYNOPSIS
yahooquote SAPG.F JARD.SI 0307.HK BII.L DIA INTC T BCE.TO CDL.NZ CTI.AX SCIL.SI TELMEXL.MX FTE.PA OLIV.MI TEF.MC VOLVA.ST EBTP4.SA
yahooquote --verbose T BCE.TO DTEGN.F

DESCRIPTION
This scripts provides a simple example for the use of Finance::YahooQuote as well as a simple check means of downloading a quote, or checking a symbol.
