NAME
fspanel - a minimalist panel for X

SYNOPSIS
fspanel

OPTIONS
None.

DESCRIPTION
This manual page documents briefly the fspanel command.

fspanel is a minimalist panel for X. It works with any Gnome compliant window manager and displays a clock and the programs. It does not providevide menus, program launchers or
applets.

Click to the far right and left of the panel to make it hide and jump to the top of the screen. Click on the arrows on the left to switch desktops.

ENVIRONMENT
None.
