NAME

foo-yc20 - YC-20 organ emulation

SYNOPSIS

foo-yc20

DESCRIPTION

The YC-20 is a divide-down combo organ designed in the late 60's. This emulation faithfully copies the features, sounds and flaws of the original organ.
