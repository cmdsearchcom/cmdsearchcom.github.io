NAME

bzrp - just like bzr, except pipe standard out to a sensible pager
 

SYNOPSIS

bzrp [COMMAND] [ARGS]
 

DESCRIPTION

bzrp is really just an alias for the bzr(1) command, except that its output is piped to sensible-pager(1).
 

SEE ALSO

bzr(1), sensible-pager(1)
