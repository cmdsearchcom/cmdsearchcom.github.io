NAME

etraining - train AUGUSTUS models

SYNOPSIS

etraining [parameters] --species=SPECIES trainfilename

OPTIONS

'trainfilename' is the filename (including relative path) to the file in genbank format containing the training sequences. These can be multi-gene sequences. SPECIES is a name for your species.

Further parameters:

--/genbank/verbosity=n
Choose one of 0,1,2 or 3. The larger the verbosity, the more (error) messages you get.

AUTHORS
