NAME
coccinella - a Jabber based IM client with integrated whiteboard

SYNOPSIS
coccinella

DESCRIPTION
The-Coccinella (coccinella) is a communication tool with a built-in whiteboard for improved collaboration with other people. The-Coccinella is available cross-platform (Windows,
MacOS X, Linux, NetBSD).

Its special feature--the built-in whiteboard--allows people to exchange multimedia elements like graphics, sketches, video or audio files while being in an online discussion.

The-Coccinella uses the XMPP (Jabber) protocol for inter-client communication and data exchange.
