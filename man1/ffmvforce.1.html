NAME
ffmvforce - force orientation test for force-feedback devices

SYNOPSIS
ffmvforce <device> [-u <update rate>]

DESCRIPTION
ffmvforce generates a force in a given direction, indicated by the position of the mouse pointer in relation to the center of the tool’s window.

Beware, the stress test may damage your device!

OPTIONS
<device>
The device to test.

-u <update rate>
The update rate in Hz (5 by default).

SEE ALSO
ffcfstress(1), fftest(1), jstest(1).
