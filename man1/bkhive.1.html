BKHIVE(1) General Commands Manual BKHIVE(1)

NAME

bkhive dumps the syskey bootkey from a Windows NT/2K/XP/Vista system hive.

SYNOPSIS

bkhive <system hive> <key file>

EXAMPLE

bkhive /mnt/ntfs/WINDOWS/system32/config/system out
