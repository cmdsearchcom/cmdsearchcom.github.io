NAME

oadm policy who-can - List who can perform the specified action on a resource
 
 

SYNOPSIS

oadm policy who-can [OPTIONS]
 
 

DESCRIPTION

List who can perform the specified action on a resource
 
 

OPTIONS

--all-namespaces=false
If true, list who can perform the specified action in all namespaces.
