NAME
ldapaddusertogroup - adds a member to a group in LDAP.

SYNOPSIS
ldapaddusertogroup <username|uid|dn> <groupname|gid>

OPTIONS
<username | uid | dn>
The name or the uid of the user to add. It can be a machine name (with an ending $) or a user name. When working with groupOfNames or groupOfUniqueNames group entries, a
full DN can also be provided.

<groupname | gid>
The name or the gid of the group that should welcome the new user.

SEE ALSO
ldapdeleteuserfromgroup(1), ldapsetprimarygroup(1), ldapscripts(5).
