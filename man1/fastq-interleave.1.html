NAME

fastq-interleave - Read 2 fastq files, and write them interleaved

SYNOPSIS

fastq-interleave x.fastq y.fastq

DESCRIPTION

Read 2 fastq files, and write them interleaved. Assumes 1 fastq per 4 lines, i.e. no line wrapping.
