NAME
elixir-compose - reformat ’elixir compose’ output

DESCRIPTION
elixir-compose can be used for reformatting the general output of elixir compose and produces the contents of lib/ElixirFM/Data/Compose.PL which is used in
lib/ElixirFM/Resolve.pm.
