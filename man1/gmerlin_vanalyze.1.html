NAME
gmerlin_vanalyze - compare two videos

SYNOPSIS
gmerlin_vanalyze <video1> <video2>

DESCRIPTION
gmerlin_vanalyze will calculate the PSNR between <video1> and <video2>.

gmerlin_vanalyze is part of the Gmerlin suite of media applications.
